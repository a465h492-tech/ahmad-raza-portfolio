Top 10 Most Famous Games

The most iconic and influential video games of all time

#1

Tetris

Puzzle

Created by Alexey Pajitnov in 1984, Tetris is the best-selling puzzle game of all time. Its simple yet addictive gameplay — arranging falling tetrominoes to complete lines — has made it a timeless classic on virtually every platform ever created.

Released: 1984 | Developer: Alexey Pajitnov
#2

Minecraft

Sandbox / Survival

The best-selling video game in history, Minecraft lets players explore a blocky, procedurally generated world, mine resources, craft tools, and build anything imaginable. Its creative freedom and survival mode have captivated over 200 million players worldwide.

Released: 2011 | Developer: Mojang Studios
#3

Grand Theft Auto V

Action-Adventure / Open World

Rockstar's masterpiece set in the sprawling city of Los Santos. With three playable protagonists, a gripping story, and the wildly popular GTA Online mode, it has generated over $8 billion in revenue — making it the most profitable entertainment product ever.

Released: 2013 | Developer: Rockstar North
#4

The Legend of Zelda: Breath of the Wild

Action-Adventure

Nintendo's groundbreaking open-air adventure redefined the Zelda franchise. Players explore the vast kingdom of Hyrule, solving puzzles, battling monsters, and uncovering secrets. Its freedom of exploration and physics-based gameplay set a new standard for open-world games.

Released: 2017 | Developer: Nintendo EPD
#5

Super Mario Bros.

Platformer

The game that saved the video game industry. Super Mario Bros. introduced tight platforming mechanics, hidden secrets, and iconic power-ups. It defined the side-scrolling genre and made Mario the most recognizable character in gaming history.

Released: 1985 | Developer: Nintendo
#6

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

RPG / Open World

CD Projekt Red's magnum opus follows Geralt of Rivia on a quest through a morally gray fantasy world. Renowned for its deep storytelling, rich characters, and meaningful choices, it remains one of the most celebrated RPGs ever made.

Released: 2015 | Developer: CD Projekt Red
#7

Red Dead Redemption 2

Action-Adventure / Western

Rockstar's stunning prequel set in the dying days of the Wild West. With unparalleled attention to detail, a deeply emotional story about outlaw Arthur Morgan, and a living, breathing open world, it set a new benchmark for immersion in gaming.

Released: 2018 | Developer: Rockstar Studios
#8

Fortnite

Battle Royale

Epic Games' cultural phenomenon that took the world by storm. With its colorful art style, building mechanics, and constant live-event updates, Fortnite became more than a game — it became a social platform and a major force in pop culture with hundreds of millions of players.

Released: 2017 | Developer: Epic Games
#9

Pokémon Red & Blue

RPG / Monster Collection

The games that started a global phenomenon. Players journey through Kanto to catch, train, and battle 151 Pokémon. Its "Gotta Catch 'Em All" mantra sparked a franchise spanning games, cards, anime, and movies — one of the highest-grossing media franchises ever.

Released: 1996 | Developer: Game Freak
#10

Elden Ring

Action RPG / Souls-like

FromSoftware's open-world epic, developed in collaboration with George R.R. Martin. Set in the vast Lands Between, players face punishing combat, explore breathtaking dungeons, and uncover a deep, fragmented lore. Winner of Game of the Year 2022 and a modern masterpiece.

Released: 2022 | Developer: FromSoftware
